About Aavas Ambassadors - Customer & Employee Lead Referral Program

Welcome to the Aavas Ambassadors - a unique referral program designed to reward our valued customers and employees for their trust and loyalty. As part of our commitment to building a stronger community, we invite you to join this exciting program where your referrals can earn you remarkable rewards and exclusive privileges.

HOW IT WORKS?

Simply refer potential leads to Aavas, and upon successful disbursement of the referred loan, you will receive exciting rewards. Our program is open to both customers and employees, ensuring everyone can benefit from their connections.

Login using registered mobile number or LAN number

Enter contact details of the person you wish to refer in a new lead

Share the Refer & Invite link through Facebook, WhatsApp, Telegram, SMS, etc. to generate the leads effortlessly

Rewards and Privileges

The Aavas Ambassadors program is designed to make your journey with us even more rewarding. As you refer more leads, you will progress through our tiered club system, gaining access to increasing levels of benefits:

  • Silver Club: Start your journey here and enjoy initial rewards for each successful referral.
  • Gold Club: Move up to the Gold Club with more referrals and unlock higher rewards and additional perks.
  • Platinum Club: Achieve Platinum status with a significant number of successful referrals and gain access to premium rewards and privileges.
  • Diamond Club: The pinnacle of our program, the Diamond Club offers the most exclusive rewards and the highest level of recognition for our top referrers.
